What are we about?

We are a coalition of many resisting fear, division, misinformation, and hate. We dissent from a corrupt government system that does not serve its citizens. We are the architects of a just future, and our tool is collective dissent.


  • Unite Movements: To unite movements for justice from labor and climate to racial and economic equality-into an unbreakable coalition, resisting the tactics of division to forge a government accountable to all.

  • Empower: To empower individuals and communities to organize general strikes, coordinate mutual aid, and demand systemic justice through peaceful mass non-compliance. We leverage technology and decentralized coordination to amplify the voices of the marginalized and hold power accountable.
  • It Sets the Agenda: We unite the voices of the unheard and ignored, dissenting from a system that prioritizes profit over people. Our movement organizes to build a future of shared prosperity, universal rights, and a government accountable to its citizens, not its donors.

  • Tools: To provide the tools for peaceful mass non-compliance. We exist to dismantle systems of greed and build a world where economic justice, human rights, and true democracy are not just ideals, but the reality for all.
  • Our Mission make justice non-negotiable, grounded in the belief that a living wage, a home, and healthcare are fundamental human rights being actively denied. We organize to end the era of systemic greed through coordinated, peaceful dissent.
