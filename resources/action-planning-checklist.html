So, You Want to Plan an Action?

Awesome. Let's make sure it's more 'history-making' and less 'disorganized-shouting-match'.

Feeling that fire in your belly? Tired of yelling at the TV? Good. That energy is the engine of change. But an engine without a steering wheel just goes in circles. That's where planning comes in.

Organizing a protest, a walkout, or any kind of civil disobedience can feel daunting. It seems like there are a million things to remember. And there are! But don't sweat it. We've broken it down into manageable chunks. Think of this as your recipe for a successful action. Follow the steps, and you'll be well on your way.
